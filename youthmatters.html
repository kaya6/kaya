Sesona Sibane Non-profit Organisation

Sesona Sibane HIV/AIDS Project was established in 2005 with the aim of tackling challenges faced by society such as illnesses, malnutrition and peer support. The organisation conducts HIV/AIDS & TB Care Project, Craft & Beading Project and Sewing Programme. We are also engaged in After-school Ex- extramural Activities and Youth After-care Programme.

SOS Children's Village

The organization provides humanitarian and developmental assistance to children in need and protects their interests and rights around the world

Vocal Revolutionaries

Vocal Revs is a youth organisation that works closely with township youth empowering them with digital skills, the organisation be- lieves in providing the disadvantaged youth of South Africa with a space of self- expression, educational improvement and a home for safety.

Youth Matters

Youth Matters Movement is a socio-economic movement by young people for young people.

Concepts by Kaya

Concepts by Kaya is an accessories brand - the brand weds both art and fashion by strictly using recycled materials to create eccentric pieces installing allusions of afro-modernism and Afro- symbolism. Through this collaboration i aims to breed a lifestyle and culture that will spark minds of influence, through this we aspire to inspire and enkindle young individuals and induce a culture of solution based thinking and independence - as young women that resonates and inspires a young person to dream and desire a life beyond their circumstances.
